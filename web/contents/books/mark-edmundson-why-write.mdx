Why write when it sometimes feels that so few people really read — read as if their lives might be changed by what they’re reading? Why write when the world wants to be informed, not enlightened; to be entertained, not inspired? 

In this book, the author, Mark Edmundson, gives several good reasons for writing, in this day and age. If the mind is a muscle, reading is its diet, and writing is its exercise.
